SUMMARY:
Dr. Puneet Kaur Kohli has been appointed Chief Technology Officer at Generali Central Life Insurance, where she will lead the company’s technology strategy and digital transformation agenda. She brings more than 30 years of multi-industry IT experience, spanning project delivery, IT strategy, cloud, data, and engineering-led modernization across insurance and financial services. Prior to this role, she served at Liberty General Insurance in dual mandates as President – Information Technology & Data and President – Chief Information & Technology Officer, and was also the Market Technology Leader for India within Liberty’s Asia Retail Markets group. At Liberty, she led large-scale modernization initiatives including cloud adoption, core systems transformation, and data-driven decision-making capabilities. In her new position, Dr. Kohli is expected to focus on strengthening Generali Central Life Insurance’s digital capabilities, enhancing customer experience, and driving innovation in line with the company’s growth and modernization objectives. The appointment underscores the insurer’s emphasis on technology leadership as a key enabler of its strategic priorities.
